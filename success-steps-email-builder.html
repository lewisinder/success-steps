Success Steps Email Builder

Personalise the email for one person, then paste it straight into Gmail. Completed steps are shown ticked-off and greyed out, so each person sees clearly what they still need to do.

How to use:  1. (Optional) type the person's first name.  2. Mark any steps they've already done.  3. Click Copy email.  4. In Gmail, open a new message and paste (Cmd/Ctrl+V) — the styling comes across.
